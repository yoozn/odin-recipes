Rainbow Cupcakes Recipe

Everyone loves cupcakes; but rainbow cupcakes are even better. With a little bit of food dye, you can transform your otherwise mediocre baking into something... unique.

Ingredients

  • 5/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 large eggs, room temp
  • red, blue, green, yellow food colouring

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line two 12-cup muffin pans with paper baking cups.
  2. Stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk together milk, begetable oil, and vanilla in seperate bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter in large bowl until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, allowing each to blend. Pour flour mixture alternately with the milk mixture, mixing until incorporated.
  4. Divide cake batter into four seperate bowls. Add few drops of food colouring into one bowl of batterand stir. Repeat with other bowls of mixture.
  5. Using a different sppoon for each batter, spoon a smalll portion into each liner.
  6. Bake in oven for 15 - 20 minutes.
