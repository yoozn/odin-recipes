Thai Beef Salad Recipe

Description

This thai recipe is a healthy way to get both protein and vegetables into your diet. It is highly nutritious, and most of the prep can be done in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lime juice
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup fish sauce
  • 2 chopped green onions
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, smashed & cut
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chili sauce
  • 3/2 pounds steak fillet
  • 1 head leaf lettuce, bite sized pieces
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved

Steps

  1. Mix lime juice, cilantro, mint leaves, sugar, fish sauce, green onions, lemongrass, chili sauce together in bowl until sugar is dissolved. Set aside.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, oil the grate.
  3. Cook steak on grill until it firms, and is slightly pink in center. About 5 minutes each side. The internal temperature of meat should be 140 degrees F.
  4. Slice streak into thin strips, and add into bowl. Refrigerate for ~3 hours.
  5. Place lettuce into a bowl, place cucumber on top. Add steakk and herb mixture. Scatter cherry tomatoes ontop.
