Strawberry Lemonade Slushy Recipe

Description

This thirst quenching drink is the perfect fruity refresher for the summer heat.

Using fresh fruit instead of sugar extracts allows for a healthy replacement to 7/11's famous alternative

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/3 cup dry lemonade-flavoured drink mix
  • 3 cups ice cubes
  • 1 cup strawberries

Steps

  1. Stir water and lemonade mix together in cup until fully dissolved.
  2. Combine ice cubes and strawberries in a blender. Pour lemonade mixture into blender. Blend on high for 10 seconds, and finish stirring with a spoon.
